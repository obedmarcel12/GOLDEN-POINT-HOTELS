About Golden Point

A Refined Hotel Experience in the Heart of Enugu

Golden Point Hotel and Suites was created for guests who value comfort, privacy, hospitality, and a stay that feels carefully prepared from the moment they arrive.

Who We Are

Built for Comfort, Class, and Memorable Hospitality

Golden Point Hotel and Suites stands as one of Enugu’s distinguished hospitality destinations, serving guests who expect more than just a room. The hotel brings together elegant accommodation, attentive service, peaceful surroundings, and modern convenience for both business and leisure travellers.

From well-appointed rooms to warm guest care, every detail is shaped around one purpose: making each stay comfortable, secure, and worthy of return. Whether a guest is visiting Enugu for work, relaxation, family events, or a private retreat, Golden Point provides an atmosphere that feels polished, welcoming, and dependable.

Our History

A Story of Growth, Trust, and Excellence

Golden Point Hotel and Suites began with a clear vision: to create a hotel in Enugu where guests could enjoy premium comfort without losing the warmth of genuine Nigerian hospitality. The idea was not simply to build another place to sleep, but to create a destination where people could arrive, settle in, and feel properly attended to.

Over time, the hotel became known for its calm environment, welcoming service, and dependable accommodation experience. Guests came not only for the rooms, but for the feeling of ease that surrounded their stay. Business travellers found a place where they could rest after demanding schedules, families found a comfortable space for visits and celebrations, and visitors to Enugu found a hotel that understood both privacy and presentation.

The growth of Golden Point has been shaped by consistency. Every room, every reception experience, every meal, and every guest interaction contributes to the hotel’s name. The brand continues to evolve with the city, improving its spaces and services while holding on to the values that made guests trust it from the beginning.

Today, Golden Point Hotel and Suites represents a confident standard of hospitality in Enugu State: elegant enough for special moments, comfortable enough for long stays, and professional enough for guests who expect things to be done properly.
